Keyboard Shortcuts

Your best friend!
Here are the ones you'll most likely use:


CTRL + A

Highlight all text in a focused area, for example when clicking inside the text boxes in BoxScan.

Try it!



CTRL + X

Used in conjunction with CTRL+A. Removes the text currently highlighted, which you can paste anywhere else.

Try it!

(Take these words on an adventure to the next box!)



CTRL + V

Paste! Used in conjunction with CTRL+C, which copies a piece of highlighted text. Unlike CTRL+X, it doesn't remove it.

Try it!



Methods



Dragging Highlighted Text

After using CTRL+A, drag the highlighted text to another text box (even on another page if you want to) to copy it over.



And we're done!
